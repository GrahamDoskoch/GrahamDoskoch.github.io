About Me

I'm a PhD student at West Virginia University, currently doing research on pulsars and pulsar timing. I graduated from Swarthmore College in 2020 with a BA in astrophysics and mathematics. At Swarthmore, I worked on x-ray spectroscopy of the winds of massive O and B stars using data from the Chandra X-ray Observatory. A paper on our group's results has been submitted and is undergoing peer review. I also did an REU in the Department of Physics at Columbia University in the summer of 2019, as part of the VERITAS gamma-ray group.
